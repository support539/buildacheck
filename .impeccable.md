## Design Context

### Users
BuildaCheck serves homeowners, buyers, builders, engineers, insurers, councils, and commercial property owners who need defensible building intelligence before purchase, during construction, or in disputes. They are making high-consequence decisions and value clarity, evidence, and speed over marketing gloss.

### Brand Personality
Precise, authoritative, and modern-industrial. The interface should feel like a premium construction intelligence service: calm, confident, and technically credible without slipping into startup hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first, copper-and-slate, structured layouts, and restrained motion. BuildaCheck remains the lead brand; BuildaScan is presented as the Digital Site Intelligence module within the same ecosystem. Citadel Building Group should appear as a subtle parent-brand trust signal rather than the primary visual identity.

### Design Principles
1. Show one unified workflow rather than disconnected services.
2. Lead with evidence, defensibility, and practical outcomes.
3. Keep pricing legible and segmented by buyer intent: fixed, optional, quoted.
4. Use technical detail as proof, not decoration.
5. Preserve the existing premium construction tone while extending it for Digital Site Intelligence.
